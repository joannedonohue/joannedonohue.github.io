Background

data, product, and business.

I'm a Senior Product Manager with 15+ years turning complex ecommerce and marketing data into self-serve decision products — tools that replace analyst requests with intuitive, real-time answers.

At Mars, I owned the full product lifecycle for Project Tahoe — an enterprise analytics platform part of a $1.2B digital transformation — serving retailer account teams at Amazon, Walmart, Chewy, Kroger, and Target. Before that, I led marketing analytics at Expedia and built pricing intelligence systems at Petco.

Most recently, I've been Principal of Strategy & Operations at Sonoran Ridge Capital, applying AI and ML tools to investment due diligence and market analysis. That investor lens — evaluating competitive positioning, scalability, and demand — is now part of how I think about every product decision.

I'm targeting product and strategy roles at high-growth consumer and CPG companies where data intelligence is a core competitive advantage.
