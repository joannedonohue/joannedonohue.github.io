About

Where data
meets design
thinking

I'm Joanne Donohue, a product and GTM leader based in Scottsdale, Arizona. My career has been built at the intersection of analytics, commerce, and creative strategy — from building attribution pipelines at Expedia Group to leading a $1.2B digital transformation at Mars Petcare.

I co-founded Sonoran Ridge Capital, hold an ASU Artificial Intelligence certificate, and bring practical ML experience to every product decision. I believe the best creative work is sharpened — never dulled — by data.

Outside of work, I paint watercolors, experiment in Lightroom, and draw constantly. That creative practice isn't a side hobby — it's the lens I bring to understanding what resonates with people.
